About FEU


Far Eastern University (FEU) is one of the leading private universities in the Philippines. Established in 1928, FEU is recognized for its commitment to academic excellence, innovation, and student development. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs in fields such as business, education, engineering, architecture, nursing, medicine, and information technology.


FEU's historic Manila campus is known for its beautiful Art Deco architecture and vibrant learning environment. The university promotes leadership, community service, and holistic growth, preparing students to become competent professionals and responsible citizens.
